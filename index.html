Common Login Problems & Solutions

Password forgotten?

Use the “Forgot Username/Password” option on the sign-in page.

Account locked?

After too many wrong attempts, wait a few minutes or reset from the official site.

Browser not working?

Clear cache, update your browser, or use private/incognito mode.

Need customer support?

Only use contact numbers listed on the official Discover website.
